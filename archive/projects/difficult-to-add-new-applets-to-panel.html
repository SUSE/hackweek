    • Difficult to add new applets to panel

    Tags: Idea

    Description

    It is difficult to add new applets to new panel if there's no empty space to right click on. I suggest adding a new context menu to everything running in the panel called "Panel..." and then have a submenu which allows you to do the same things as you can do when right clicking on empty space.

    People

    Magnus Boman originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).

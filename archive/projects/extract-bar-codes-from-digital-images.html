    • Extract bar codes from digital images

    Tags: Idea, BarCode, Scan, Graphics, Library

    Description

    Write a tool which is able to extract bar codes from digital images. This would for example allow to take a picture of your DVDs with your digital camera, run the tool, automatically look up the DVDs on Amazon and import them into a DVD management application or something like that.

    Such a tool might finally enable organizationally less gifted people to keep an overview of their DVDs and stuff like that and prevent embarrassing double buys.

    People

    Cornelius Schumacher originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).

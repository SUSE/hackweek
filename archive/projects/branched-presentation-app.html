    • branched presentation app

    Tags: Idea

    Description

    A branched presentation application - Impress or Kpresenter like, that offers a branched or tree like structure. Something that could support a computer based presentation that could choose between different sets of information in a presentation. Perhaps something that links existing presentation files together in a arbitrary way.

    Something that allows going back to previous points in a presentation, and allows annotation, and addition of other branches.

    Potential use as a teaching device and or group decision/ evaluation tool

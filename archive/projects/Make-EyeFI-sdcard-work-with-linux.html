    • Make EyeFI sdcard work with linux

    Description

    EyeFi.com sells sdcards with a built in wlan client, allowing to upload your photos to a server, directly from within the camera. EyeFi cards require a full featured wlan network (not ad-hoc), and need to connect to specific servers of eyefi.com

    wine can be used to study the protocol. Goal is to build a local linux setup that works with these sdcards.

    • Accelerated Movie player

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, Mono, video, multimedia

    Description

    Create a simple video player based on gstreamer# that uses clutter# for controls and gtk#/cairo# for windowing/events and other drawings.

    Integrate best with gnome-volume-manager for best automount of dvds/vcds and showcase fullscreen video with clutter controls. Compiz currently supports a composited video plugin.

    People

    Srinivasa Ragavan originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).

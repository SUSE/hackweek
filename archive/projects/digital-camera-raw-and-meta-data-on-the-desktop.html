    • Digital camera RAW and metadata on the desktop

    Tags: Graphics, Desktop, openSUSE, SLED, Photography, InProgress

    Description

    Provide digital camera RAW file thumbnail support at the GNOME desktop level like it is done for images and video. This involve writing a thumbnailer, using libopenraw.

    Provide broader metadata support in both Nautilus and eog. That involve providing XMP support using Exempi, and eventually Exif support for other types using exiv2.

    The preliminary step is to put both libopenraw and exempi into the build service.

    Further steps would be to provide GdkPixbuf loader for the RAW files. And provide loading of preview from RAW files in eog.

    People

    Hubert Figuiere originated this idea and is working on it.

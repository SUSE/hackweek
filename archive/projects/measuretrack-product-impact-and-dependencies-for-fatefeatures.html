    • Measure/track "Product Impact" and dependencies for fate/features

    Tags: Idea, fate, devtool

    Description

    It should be more visible/clear what an impact a feature will have to the product. Mainly to avoid e.g. changing an interface without changing its corresponding yast gui, to shorten discussions - or at least to have a quick overview what has to be implemented for the specific product to have it consistent. This could happen through a tag based database e.g. "has_gui", "has_yast_module", or even through extending rpm to support more of such abilities, which would be also good for product/maintenance handling (although we currently do not respect this).

    People

    Sebastian Vollath originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).

    • Evolution Common Attachments Plugin

    Tags: Idea, Evolution, Done

    Description

    Reduce the number of steps for frequently added attachments by adding a configurable submenu of 5 or 10 files commonly sent as attachments.

    Example usage: A receptionist may send out requested forms to staff. Repetitively, s/he has to click Insert > Attachment > then browse to folder location of file needed. But if you click Insert > Attachment > (List of preconfigured attachments) you save alot of time (and memory trying to remember where those files were stored.)

    This could also be used by sales people who want to send out brochures, or job applicants who want to send out resumes, etc.

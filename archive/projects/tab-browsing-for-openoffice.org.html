    • Tab browsing for OpenOffice.org

    Tags: Desktop, office, InProgress

    Description

    Would be fantastic to introduce "Tabbed browsing" in OpenOffice.org. It would let you load all your documents (text documents, spreadsheets, slideshows) in separate tabs of a single OpenOffice.org window.

    I would love to see this prototyped to see whether or not tab browsing in the office suite would help productivity.

    Navigating across several documents while doing reviews or composing new documents could greatly be improved.

    People

    Guy Lunardi originated this idea. Fong Lin will work on implementing this idea in Hacking Week.
